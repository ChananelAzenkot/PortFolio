My Name Is Chananel

A full-stack web student is an individual learning both front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, server-side programming, and databases. They aim to become versatile professionals capable of handling all aspects of web development and delivering comprehensive solutions..

Fullstack web development requires patience, persistence, and a willingness to adapt to new technologies. Skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Angular, Node.js or Ruby on Rails are important, as well as communication, collaboration, prioritization, and task management skills. Dedication and hard work lead to success in building amazing web application.
